## TurboTax Free Edition: Free for simple tax returns
**Approximately 37% of taxpayers qualify for the TurboTax Free Edition.** If your tax situation is more straightforward, you may qualify for [TurboTax Free Edition](https://turbotax.intuit.com/personal-taxes/online/free-edition.jsp?priorityCode=3468337910&cid=em_38922_6701_003_2017), which allows eligible filers to file both federal and state tax returns for $0.
_“If you have a Simple Form 1040 return only (no schedules, except for EITC, CTC, student loan interest, and Schedule 1-A), you can file both your federal and state taxes for free with TurboTax Free Edition. About 37% of filers qualify. If you have a more involved tax return and you are new to TurboTax, you can file your federal and state taxes for free using TurboTax online_.” said CPA and tax expert with TurboTax, Lisa Greene-Lewis.
**You’ll likely qualify if:**
  * Your income comes from a [W-2](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/irs-tax-forms/what-is-a-w-2-form/L6VJbqWl5?priorityCode=3468337910)
  * You take the [standard deduction](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/tax-deductions-and-credits/what-are-standard-tax-deductions/L7oiVM1DH?priorityCode=3468337910&cid=em_38922_6701_003_2017)
  * You have limited interest or dividends that don’t require a [Schedule B](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/investments-and-taxes/what-is-a-schedule-b-irs-form/L9G2RijlF?priorityCode=3468337910)
  * You claim common credits like the [Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/tax-deductions-and-credits/earned-income-credit/L4lmLBNWa?priorityCode=3468337910&cid=em_38922_6701_003_2017), [Child Tax Credit (CTC)](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/family/child-tax-credit/L9ZIjdlZz?priorityCode=3468337910&cid=em_38922_6701_003_2017), [Student Loan Interest Deduction](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/college-and-education/about-student-loan-tax-credits/L0mIiywan?priorityCode=3468337910&cid=em_38922_6701_003_2017), or [Schedule 1-A](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/tax-deductions-and-credits/what-is-irs-schedule-1-a-new-2025-form-for-additional-deductions-explained/c3EtLSv3w?priorityCode=3468337910&cid=em_38922_6701_003_2017)

## Free filing FAQ
**Can I switch between the mobile app and my computer?**
Yes. Your TurboTax account syncs across devices. Just make sure you start and file your return in the same product you qualify for.
**What happens if my tax situation changes mid-way?**
TurboTax adapts as you go. If you start with a simple return but need to report something more complex, you’ll be notified of any changes upfront. If you qualify for free filing, you can still file for $0 even if complexity increases, as long as you meet the eligibility requirements.
**Do free versions include the Maximum Refund Guarantee?**
Yes. Every TurboTax product — free or paid — includes the [Maximum Refund Guarantee](https://turbotax.intuit.com/corp/guarantees/?priorityCode=3468337910), using the same tax calculation engine trusted by millions of filers each year.
