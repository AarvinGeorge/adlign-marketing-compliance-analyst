# What's the difference between TurboTax’s Up to 5 Days Early Refund Delivery Options and Refund Advance loan?

When you file with TurboTax, you have three options to receive money fast: 
  * Get up to $4,000**†** instantly1 with a **Refund Advance loan** with $0 loan fees and 0% APR.
  * Get your federal refund **Up to** **5 Days Early 2** in any bank account, guaranteed3 for a fee.
  * Get your federal refund **up to 5 days early****4** in a Credit Karma MoneyTM account5 for no additional cost.


Here is a brief description of each of the fast money options:
## Up to 5 Days Early Refund Delivery
**Up to** **5 Days Early into your Preferred Bank Account:** TurboTax’s Up to 5 Days Early refund delivery gives you the flexibility to have your federal refund deposited into your preferred bank account up to 5 days earlier than the date your refund would have arrived if sent from the IRS directly. Terms apply. 
**Up to 5 Days Early - Credit Karma Money checking account:** When you open and deposit your refund into a Credit Karma Money account, your tax refund could be deposited up to 5 days early for no additional cost.
With this option, TurboTax works with Credit Karma and a partner bank to get your account information to the IRS so they can deposit your refund into your account up to five days earlier. You can use the Credit Karma refund tracker to see when your federal refund is expected to hit your account. [Learn more about the other features of a Credit Karma Money checking account](https://ttlc.intuit.com/turbotax-support/en-us/help-article/account-management/features-credit-karma-money-spend-account-offer/L3lNvwe1T_US_en_US). 
With both refund options, there are some situations where you might not be able to receive your refund early. These include not having a sufficient refund, IRS delays, or your bank’s policies. However, if you choose Up to 5 Days Early into your Preferred Bank Account, and can’t receive your refund at least a day early due to these reasons, you won’t be charged the service fee. 
Please note that you may also be able to receive your federal refund early through your bank, credit union, or other financial institution. Terms, fees, and delivery dates may vary. Check with your financial institution for details.
## **Refund Advance**
If you apply for a Refund Advance loan and are approved, you could receive your Refund Advance in as little as 60 seconds from IRS acceptance of your e-filed return. Most customers receive funds within 15 minutes of acceptance. 
Your Refund Advance funds will be deposited into your Credit Karma Money checking account, and you can start spending immediately with a virtual Credit Karma Visa® Debit Card6 anywhere Visa is accepted. A physical Credit Karma Visa® Debit Card will be mailed to you after you open the checking account inside of TurboTax. 
Refund Advance loans may be issued by First Century Bank, N.A. or WebBank. Your loan amount will be based on your anticipated federal refund to a maximum of 50% of that refund amount. The loan will be equal to a portion of your anticipated refund in one of 10 amounts: $250, $500, $750, $1,000, $1,500, $2,000, $2,500, $3,000, $3,500, or $4,000. You’ll only receive what you’ve been approved for as a Refund Advance loan.
This offer is available until February 28, 2025, or the date that available funds have been exhausted, whichever comes first.
[Learn more about the Refund Advance program](https://ttlc.intuit.com/oicms/L52Mg0G5u_US_en_US).
