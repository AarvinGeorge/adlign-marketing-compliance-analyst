## Who is eligible for free tax filing?
This [free filing](https://turbotax.intuit.com/personal-taxes/online/military-edition.jsp?priorityCode=3468337910) offer is available to **U.S. enlisted active-duty and reserve service members with ranks E-1 through E-9 who receive a W-2 from the Defense Finance and Accounting Service (DFAS)**. Eligible service members can also include a spouse on a joint return, even if the spouse is not in the military.
The offer does not apply to:
  * Commissioned officers
  * Warrant officers
  * Retirees 
  * Veterans who are no longer on active duty
  * National Guard members unless they are serving under qualifying active reserve orders through DFAS

## Frequently Asked Tax Questions for military filers
### Do I have to pay taxes on combat pay?
Combat pay is generally excluded from taxable income, which means it won’t count toward your federal income tax. However, you can choose to include it when calculating certain tax credits (like the [Earned Income Tax Credit](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/tax-deductions-and-credits/earned-income-credit/L4lmLBNWa?priorityCode=3468337910)) if it benefits your refund.
### How do I file if my spouse and I live in different states?
Military spouses often qualify for protections under the [Military Spouses Residency Relief Act](https://ttlc.intuit.com/turbotax-support/en-us/help-article/state-taxes/military-spouses-state-taxes/L9JMT8Mo3_US_en_US?priorityCode=3468337910) (MSRRA), which allows them to keep their home-state residency for tax purposes even if the service member is stationed elsewhere. TurboTax helps determine state-residency rules for both spouses so you file correctly.
### What documents should I have ready before I start?
When you get ready to file, you’ll want to have the following documents available:
  * Your military W-2
  * Any additional income documents (such as civilian employment, non-military spouse income, or investment income)
  * Last year’s return 
  * Records for deductible expenses like moving costs, education, or childcare
