Waiting for your income tax refund in _this_ economy? No, thanks.
Whether you’re planning to use your refund to fund your dream vacation, pay down debt, or get ahead on your expenses, the sooner the cash hits your bank account, the better. And it’s easier than you think to speed up the [refund process](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/tax-refund/wheres-my-tax-refund-the-irs-refund-timetable-explained/L9ZoW82cT?priorityCode=3468337910).

## **The prep: Tax filing tips for a speedy refund**
Accessing your refund ASAP starts with a smart tax filing strategy. That’s because there’s a delay between when the IRS receives your tax return and when they distribute your refund. And you can take steps to make that delay as short as possible.
### **File early and online**
Even if you’re a natural procrastinator, getting a jump-start on your tax return can help you minimize delays in getting your refund. Filing early in the tax season — before most people do their taxes — helps you get in ahead of the usual backlog, helping the IRS process your return sooner.
And, if you’re considering filing your return the old-fashioned way, using a paper return, think again. It increases the processing time to[ six weeks](https://www.taxpayeradvocate.irs.gov/get-help/refunds/expediting-a-refund/), compared to two weeks with [e-file](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/e-file/video-e-filing-for-a-faster-refund/L0Htz2kml?priorityCode=3468337910).
### **Set up direct deposit**
You can also avoid delays by setting up direct deposit to receive your refund electronically. You’ll receive payment within a few days of it being sent, depending on your financial institution, rather than waiting for your check in the mail or for the funds to clear.
### **Double-check your return**
Any errors in your return can extend its processing time — and the time it takes to get your refund — so make sure you’re filing accurate returns to avoid delays.
Don’t worry: We’re here to help you get your return right the first time.[ Learn how to avoid errors in your tax return](https://turbotax.intuit.com/tax-tips/irs-tax-return/video-how-to-reduce-errors-on-your-tax-return/L1nJXHnEy?priorityCode=3468337910).
