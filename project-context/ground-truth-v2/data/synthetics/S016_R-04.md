## Credit Karma Money™ Spend Account — Pricing & Perks

No monthly fees. No minimum balance to open. No overdraft fees. Just a smarter way to spend, save, and see your money grow.

### What it costs
$0. Zero monthly maintenance fees, zero minimum opening deposit, and zero hidden charges. That's the TurboTax way of doing banking — straightforward, transparent pricing.

### The New Member Reward
Open a new Credit Karma Money Spend account and set up qualifying direct deposits totaling **$1,000 or more within your first 60 days**, and you'll unlock a **$150 reward** added to your account balance. To stay eligible, keep at least **$500** sitting in your account on the day your qualifying deposits are confirmed. If you're aiming to also open a linked Save account, just know that account carries its own $25 opening deposit, a bit more than what's needed to qualify for the reward itself.

We tally up your qualifying deposits automatically — no forms, no phone calls, no waiting on hold. Just link your paycheck or benefits deposit and watch your progress in the app.

### Why members love it
- Early access to paycheck (up to 2 days sooner with direct deposit)
- Free access to over 55,000 Allpoint® ATMs nationwide
- Built-in budgeting tools synced with your Credit Karma score
- FDIC insurance through our partner banks, up to applicable limits

### Also available: TurboTax Free Edition & Refund Advance
File your simple taxes for $0 with TurboTax Free Edition, and if you're expecting a refund, see if you qualify for Refund Advance — a $0 loan with no interest, funded onto your Credit Karma Money account often within minutes of IRS acceptance.

Love the app? Share your referral link. When a friend opens an account and meets the deposit requirement above, you both get rewarded. Terms and conditions apply; rewards are subject to change without notice.
