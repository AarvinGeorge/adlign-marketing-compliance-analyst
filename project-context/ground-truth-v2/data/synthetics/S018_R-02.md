## Compare Ways to Pay for Your Taxes

Filing with TurboTax gives you flexibility. Take a look at how our payment options stack up, so you can pick what fits your budget this season.

### TurboTax Free Edition
~37% of filers qualify. Simple tax returns only (Form 1040, no schedules) — file federal and state for $0. No hidden fees, no surprises.

### Pay Now
Pay your TurboTax fees upfront with a credit or debit card at checkout. This is the simplest way to go — no waiting, no financing, no extra charges.

### Pay Later Plan
Don't want to pay today? Split your TurboTax fees into smaller payments with our Pay Later Plan, offered through a third-party lender. Just a low 1.5% monthly rate on your unpaid balance until it's paid in full. Payments are automatically deducted from your refund or bank account on your chosen schedule.

### Refund Advance
Get up to $4,000 of your anticipated refund, typically within minutes of IRS acceptance, at no extra cost, disbursed to a Credit Karma Money™ checking account. No loan fees, and 0% interest — this is not the same as our Pay Later financing option above.

### Credit Karma Money
Open a Credit Karma Money™ checking account to receive your refund up to 5 days early, with no monthly fees and no minimum balance requirements.

### Refer a Friend
Know someone who needs to file? Share your unique referral link. When they file with TurboTax, you both could earn a reward — check the referral hub in your account for details and current offer terms.

---

**Which option is right for you?** Most filers choose Pay Now for simplicity, but if you'd rather spread out the cost, our Pay Later Plan lets you defer payment while your return gets processed. Terms, eligibility, and lender approval apply. See full plan disclosures before enrolling.

*TurboTax Free Edition, Refund Advance, and Credit Karma Money offers are subject to eligibility requirements. Refund Advance is a loan offered by a partner bank, member FDIC, subject to approval. Credit Karma Money accounts are opened by MVB Bank, Inc., Member FDIC. Terms apply.*
