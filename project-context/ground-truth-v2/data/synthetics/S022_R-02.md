## Tired of Waiting on Your Refund? We Get It.

Filing season is stressful enough without watching your bank account for days on end. That's why TurboTax offers **Refund Advance**, a no-cost way to access up to $4,000 of your anticipated federal refund, often within minutes of IRS acceptance.

### How It Works

1. File your federal return with TurboTax Free Edition or any TurboTax paid product.
2. Apply for Refund Advance during the filing process — it only takes a few extra clicks.
3. If approved, funds are typically loaded onto a Credit Karma Money™ account the same day.
4. When your actual refund arrives, it simply pays off the advance automatically.

### What Does It Cost?

We know you want the full picture before you decide. Here's the simple version: for every $100 you borrow through Refund Advance, your cost works out to just a few cents a month while the advance is outstanding — a small convenience charge built into the offer, not a separate bill you'll ever see. Most customers pay off their advance within a few weeks once the IRS releases their refund, so the total dollar cost is usually just a couple of dollars.

There's no credit check that impacts your score, no hidden fees, and no impact if your refund ends up being different than expected — TurboTax handles the reconciliation for you.

### Why TurboTax Customers Love It

- **Speed:** Funds often arrive same-day.
- **Simplicity:** No separate application, no extra paperwork.
- **Transparency:** We tell you the small monthly cost upfront in plain dollars and cents, not confusing bank jargon.
