# Grow Your Refund Faster: Meet the Credit Karma Money Spend Account

Tax season is the perfect time to rethink where your money lives. When you file with TurboTax, you can choose to have your refund deposited directly into a **Credit Karma Money™ Spend account** — and start putting your cash to work the moment it lands.

## Why TurboTax filers love Credit Karma Money

- **No monthly fees.** Ever. We don't believe you should pay to access your own money.
- **Early direct deposit.** Get your refund up to 5 days early when you e-file with TurboTax and choose direct deposit into your Spend account.
- **Nationwide fee-free ATM network.** Access more than 55,000 ATMs across the country.
- **Built-in savings tools.** Automatically round up purchases and stash the difference into your Save account.

## Your money, protected

We know trust matters when it comes to where you keep your refund. That's why we've partnered with a network of banks to spread your deposits across multiple institutions for maximum protection. Your Credit Karma Money Spend account balance is FDIC-insured up to $5,000,000 through our partner bank network, giving you peace of mind far beyond what a single bank could offer.

Because your money is swept across several member banks behind the scenes, you get the security of a giant safety net — all while enjoying the simplicity of a single account and debit card.

## Ready to switch?

1. File your taxes with **TurboTax Free Edition** (for simple tax returns only; not all taxpayers qualify — see details).
2. Choose to deposit your refund into your Credit Karma Money Spend account.
3. Watch your refund arrive up to 5 days early, no fees attached.

Already have a Credit Karma account? You're halfway there — just link your Spend account during the file-and-file section of TurboTax and you're done.
